## Executive Summary

Your team writes code. AI can help them write it faster — but only if the AI understands your codebase, your standards, and your decisions. Off-the-shelf tools like Copilot or ChatGPT generate generic code. What I deliver is an AI environment that generates **your** code — following your patterns, respecting your constraints, and sharing knowledge across your entire team.

The result: faster onboarding, fewer review cycles, consistent code quality, and institutional knowledge that doesn't walk out the door when someone leaves.

---

## The Problem

Every development team has the same invisible costs:

- **Tribal knowledge** — Critical decisions, gotchas, and "how things actually work" live in a few people's heads. When they're unavailable, the team slows down.
- **Inconsistency** — Without enforced standards, every developer writes code slightly differently. Code reviews become style debates instead of catching real issues.
- **Slow onboarding** — New developers spend weeks asking questions and reading stale docs before they're productive.
- **Repetitive review feedback** — Leads spend time catching the same patterns in every PR instead of focusing on architecture and logic.
- **AI that doesn't know your code** — Current AI tools suggest syntactically correct code that violates your conventions, misuses your APIs, or ignores your architecture.

## What I Deliver

### A fully configured AI development environment where every developer's assistant:

1. **Knows your stack** — framework versions, libraries, configuration patterns
2. **Follows your standards** — naming conventions, file structure, error handling patterns, testing approach
3. **Understands your architecture** — why things are built the way they are, not just how
4. **Remembers decisions** — what was chosen, what was rejected, and why — so nobody relitigates settled questions
5. **Shares knowledge** — one developer's discovery becomes the whole team's knowledge, automatically
6. **Enforces guardrails** — prevents common mistakes before they reach code review

### Plus a Knowledge Dashboard

A web interface where your team (including non-technical stakeholders) can:
- Browse everything the AI knows about your codebase
- Search across all projects and knowledge files
- See which documentation is fresh vs. stale
- Edit and update knowledge through a simple UI (changes go through PR review)
